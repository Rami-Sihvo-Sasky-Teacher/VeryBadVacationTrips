Discover Kuopio

Kuopio is a vibrant city located in the heart of the Finnish Lakeland. With a population of approximately 120,000, it's the eighth largest city in Finland. Known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and high quality of life, Kuopio offers a perfect blend of urban amenities and serene landscapes.

Experience Kuopio

Discover the charm of Kuopio, from its beautiful lakes to its modern city life. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, Kuopio has something for everyone.
